Objective: Continuously track and refresh relevant open roles across job boards, so the platform always has a high-quality pool to match against.

JTBD: When a user's preferences are set, the platform finds and keeps relevant listings current in the background, so Discover always draws from a live, filtered set.

JTBD: When I open the app, I want a curated, scored list ready for me, so I can act on the best opportunities immediately.

## Apply

Objective: Raise application quality and referral hit rate while cutting time spent on logistics.
