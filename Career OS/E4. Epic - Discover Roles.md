
# Epic: Discover Roles

[[B. Value chain with objectives]]

## Objective

Serve a daily ranked list of roles matched to stated preferences, so users spend zero time filtering noise.

## User Stories

### 1. Relevant Jobs / Match Score / Curated Daily Stack (swipe)

As a job seeker, I want to swipe through a daily curated stack of job cards ranked by match score, so that I can quickly act on the best opportunities without any manual searching.

Swipe model (industry best practice — right swipe is a high-intent action, not a passive save):
- Swipe right = "interested": the role is shortlisted AND the Apply flow is initiated for it (surface potential referees via affinity match + draft a referral message — see Apply). This makes the right swipe move the role toward action, not just into a backlog.
- Swipe left = "not interested": the role is dismissed.
- Both swipes are recorded as positive/negative preference signals that update the Candidate Brief, so the stack gets smarter over time (the yes/no learning loop).

Acceptance Criteria:
- Each job card shows role title, company, location, work mode, and match score.
- Swiping right shortlists the role and triggers the Apply flow; swiping left dismisses it. Every swipe feeds a signal back to the Candidate Brief.
- The daily stack is ranked by match score (highest first) and refreshes automatically each day.
- The stack respects a defined daily limit; when the user reaches the end of the stack, they see a clear "you're all caught up for today" state rather than an empty screen.

---

### 2. Paste Any URL You Want Into Your Stack

As a job seeker, I want to paste any job listing URL directly into the platform, so that I can track roles I find outside the platform alongside my matched recommendations.

Acceptance Criteria:
- Platform extracts and stores key job details (title, company, location, work mode) from the pasted URL. (similar to a job opening ingested from a new job listing saved in the platform)
- The pasted role is scored with a match score and supports the same Apply flow (referee surfacing + referral draft) and tracking as platform-matched roles.
- Unsupported or broken URLs show a clear error message with guidance.

---
